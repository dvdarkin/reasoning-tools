# Persuasion & Influence

Reasoning tools for changing minds and shaping behavior.

## Domains in This Category

1. **Game Theory (Signaling)** - Strategic communication, credible commitments, signaling costs
2. **Social Psychology** - Cognitive biases, social proof, commitment/consistency
3. **Classical Rhetoric** - Ethos, pathos, logos, rhetorical devices

## Common Themes

- Understanding what makes messages persuasive
- Recognizing psychological triggers and biases
- Building credibility and trust
- Structuring arguments for maximum impact
- Adapting communication to audience and context

## Cross-Domain Tools

Tools that appear across multiple domains in this category:
- Framing effects
- Social proof and consensus
- Commitment and consistency
- Scarcity and urgency
- Authority and credibility signals
